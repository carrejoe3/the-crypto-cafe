Which cryptocurrencies do you accept as payment?

We currently accepted Bitcoin, Ethereum, Litecoin and Bitcoin Cash, although we are planning on accepting more in the future.

Whats the average shipping time?

Orders ship within 3-5 business days and are shipped via Ground.

Can I cancel an order?

Please send us an email at info@lecryptocafe.com if you need to cancel an order. You must cancel within 12 hours of placement of the order.

What are the shipping costs?

International shipping charges are based on weight and are the least expensive option via USPS (United States Postal Service). We do not charge for shipping to the USA.

Do you accept returns?

Returned coffee is not accepted as it is a perishable item, unless there was an issue such as a damaged delivery. Any express shipping where applicable is not refunded. Express shipping is Next Day Air, 2nd Day Air, 3 Day Select and other overnight or express shipping services that may have been requested by you. Ground shipping is not refunded unless our shipping department was at fault and your order arrived damaged. If you receive your order and notice the order is damaged you must contact us within 48 hours for an exchange. No exchanges or returns will be accepted after 48 hours.
